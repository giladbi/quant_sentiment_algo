SAN FRANCISCO (MarketWatch) — Among the companies whose shares are expected to see active trade in Thursday’s session are Exxon Mobil Corp., New York Times Co., and American International Group Inc.

Exxon /quotes/zigman/203975/delayed/quotes/nls/xom XOM +1.34% is projected to report third-quarter earnings of $1.77 a share, according to a consensus survey by FactSet. The company’s board on Wednesday declared a fourth-quarter dividend of 63 cents a share, unchanged from the third quarter. The dividend will be paid on Dec. 10 to shareholders of record at the close of business on Nov. 12.

New York Times /quotes/zigman/235682/delayed/quotes/nls/nyt NYT -1.85% is forecast to post a loss of 3 cents a share in the third quarter. The media group last week said it completed the sale of the New England Media Group to a company owned by John Henry, owner of Fenway Sports Group, for $70 million. New York Times said it plans to use the proceeds for general corporate purposes.

AIG /quotes/zigman/557836/delayed/quotes/nls/aig AIG -1.45%  is expected to report third-quarter earnings of 96 cents a share. Analysts at Keefe, Bruyette & Woods recently said insurers like AIG are likely to post strong underwriting results in the third quarter due to mostly favorable global weather.

/quotes/zigman/9962609/delayed/quotes/nls/fb FB 49.12, -0.99, -1.97%
Facebook Inc.

After Wednesday’s closing bell, Facebook Inc. /quotes/zigman/9962609/delayed/quotes/nls/fb FB -1.97%  said it earned 25 cents a share on an adjusted basis in the third quarter, beating the average consensus of 19 cents a share. Facebook’s stock surged 12% in after-hours trading.

Expedia Inc. /quotes/zigman/7619798/delayed/quotes/nls/expe EXPE +0.39%  reported its third-quarter profit rose to $1.22 a share from $1.20 a share a year earlier. On an adjusted basis, earnings rose 8% to $1.43 a share, above Wall Street’s forecast of $1.36 share. Expedia shares jumped 14% in after hours.

Starbucks Corp. /quotes/zigman/20720/delayed/quotes/nls/sbux SBUX -1.05%  said its fourth-quarter profit came in at 63 cents a share, beating the consensus estimate of 60 cents a share. The board also raised the quarterly dividend by 24% to 26 cents a share. The stock fell 2.3% in after hours.

Crocs Inc. /quotes/zigman/223454/delayed/quotes/nls/cr CR +0.25%  said it earned 18 cents a share on an adjusted basis in the third quarter, slightly below the 19 cents a share projected by analysts. The company also announced that the board on Tuesday expanded the existing stock buyback plan by 15 million shares. The footwear company’s stock edged up 0.2% in late trading.

Notable companies slated to report earnings on Thursday are as follow.

ConocoPhillips /quotes/zigman/294662/delayed/quotes/nls/cop COP +1.83% is likely to post third-quarter earnings of $1.46 a share.

The total value of mergers and acquisitions in Asia Pacific has dropped by 11% this year, but companies have plenty of cash and are looking for opportunities to spend it.

MasterCard Inc. /quotes/zigman/390906/delayed/quotes/nls/ma MA -0.34%  is projected to report earnings of $6.94 a share in the third quarter. Analysts at J.P. Morgan, however, believe MasterCard’s earnings may come in above consensus estimate on slower operating expense growth and lower rebate assumption.

Beauty products company Estée Lauder Cos. /quotes/zigman/130587/delayed/quotes/nls/el EL +0.71%  is forecast to post fiscal first-quarter earnings of 73 cents a share while Avon Products Inc. /quotes/zigman/218767/delayed/quotes/nls/avp AVP +0.51%  is likely to post third-quarter earnings of 19 cents a share.

Time Warner Cable Inc. /quotes/zigman/528868/delayed/quotes/nls/twc TWC +0.20%  is expected to report third-quarter earnings of $1.66 a share.

Cigna Corp. /quotes/zigman/222255/delayed/quotes/nls/ci CI +1.13%  is forecast to post third-quarter earnings of $1.62 a share.

Clorox Co. /quotes/zigman/222530/delayed/quotes/nls/clx CLX +1.49% is projected to post earnings of $1 a share in the fiscal first quarter.

MGM Resorts International /quotes/zigman/233898/delayed/quotes/nls/mgm MGM -0.26% is likely to report a loss of 3 cents a share in the third quarter.

Beam Inc. /quotes/zigman/6577531/delayed/quotes/nls/beam BEAM +2.39%  is expected to post third-quarter earnings of 58 cents a share.

Newmont Mining Corp. /quotes/zigman/235723/delayed/quotes/nls/nem NEM +2.08%  is forecast to post earnings of 32 cents a share in the third quarter.

First Solar Inc. /quotes/zigman/102025/delayed/quotes/nls/fslr FSLR -1.56% is projected to post third-quarter earnings of $1.13 a share. “We believe [projects] backlog continues to deteriorate, and see 2014 as a bridge year, with pipeline conversion, technology implementation, and better utilization necessary for long-term success,” Robert Stone, an analyst at Cowen & Co., said in a report.
