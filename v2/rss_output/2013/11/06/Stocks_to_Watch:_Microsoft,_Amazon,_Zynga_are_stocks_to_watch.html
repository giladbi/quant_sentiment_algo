SAN FRANCISCO (MarketWatch) — Among the companies whose shares are expected to see active trade in Friday’s session are Microsoft Corp., Amazon.com Inc., and Zynga Inc.

After Thursday’s closing bell, Microsoft /quotes/zigman/20493/delayed/quotes/nls/msft MSFT +4.20%  said its fiscal first-quarter profit rose to 62 cents a share from 53 cents a share a year ago. Shares of the software giant rallied over 5% in after-hours trading.

Amazon.com /quotes/zigman/63011/delayed/quotes/nls/amzn AMZN -0.76%  late Thursday announced its third-quarter net loss narrowed to 9 cents a share from a loss of 60 cents a share a year ago while revenue jumped 24% to $17.09 billion. Amazon shares surged 7.8% in after hours.

/quotes/zigman/7720406/delayed/quotes/nls/znga ZNGA 3.71, -0.10, -2.50%
Zynga Inc.

Zynga /quotes/zigman/7720406/delayed/quotes/nls/znga ZNGA -2.50%  reported a loss of $68,000 in the third quarter, compared with a loss of $52.7 million a year ago. On a per-share basis, the company broke even versus a loss of 7 cents a share. Excluding items, the social game maker lost 2 cents a share in the quarter, better than the 4-cents-a-share loss forecast by analysts. Zynga shares soared over 9% in after hours.

United Parcel Service Inc. /quotes/zigman/251269/delayed/quotes/nls/ups UPS -1.20%  is projected to report fourth-quarter earnings of 52 cents a share, according to a consensus survey by Thomson Reuters.

Moody’s Corp. /quotes/zigman/267181/delayed/quotes/nls/mco MCO +0.24%  is forecast to post third-quarter earnings of 82 cents a share.

Procter & Gamble Co. /quotes/zigman/238894/delayed/quotes/nls/pg PG +1.69% is expected to report first-quarter earnings of $1.05 a share.

Legg Mason Inc. /quotes/zigman/231945/delayed/quotes/nls/lm LM +0.69%  is likely to report 61 cents a share in the second quarter.

National Oilwell Varco Inc. /quotes/zigman/169349/delayed/quotes/nls/nov NOV +1.34%  is projected to post third-quarter earnings of $1.32 a share.
