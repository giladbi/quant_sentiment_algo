By Sue Chang, MarketWatch

SAN FRANCISCO (MarketWatch) — Among the companies whose shares are expected to see active trade in Thursday’s session are Twitter Inc., Whole Foods Market Inc., Walt Disney Co., and Groupon Inc.

Twitter /quotes/zigman/23556538/delayed/quotes/nls/twtr TWTR -7.24%  is expected to draw a lot of the market’s attention Thursday as it debuts on the New York Stock Exchange. The social media company priced its initial public offering of 70 million shares at $26 a share to raise as much as $2 billion. The IPO price is above the range of $23 to $25 set by the company earlier this week. Twitter also granted the underwriters a 30-day option to buy up to 10.5 million in additional shares.

After Wednesday’s closing bell, Whole Foods /quotes/zigman/80039/delayed/quotes/nls/wfm WFM +2.25% reported its fiscal fourth-quarter earnings rose 7% to $121 million, or 32 cents a share. However, revenue totaled $2.98 billion, below the $3.04 billion forecast by analysts in a Thomson Reuters survey. Whole Foods shares tumbled more than 8% in after-hours trading.

On Thursday, Walt Disney /quotes/zigman/245568/delayed/quotes/nls/dis DIS +2.13%  is projected to report fourth-quarter earnings of 76 cents a share, according to a consensus survey by FactSet. The stock’s price target was raised to $77 from $65 at Wunderlich Securities earlier this week.

Groupon /quotes/zigman/7212269/delayed/quotes/nls/grpn GRPN +6.43%  is forecast to post third-quarter earnings of a penny a share. Analysts at Sterne Agee reiterated the stock’s buy rating on Tuesday. “We think GRPN continues to benefit from two powerful secular eCommerce trends -- local and mobile,” Arvind Bhatia at Sterne Agee said in a report.

Priceline.com Inc. /quotes/zigman/90481/delayed/quotes/nls/pcln PCLN +4.92%  is expected to report third-quarter earnings of $16.22 a share.

Apollo Global Management LLC /quotes/zigman/4560597/delayed/quotes/nls/apo APO -1.50%  is likely to post third-quarter earnings of 94 cents a share.

Clear Channel Outdoor Holdings Inc. /quotes/zigman/396374/delayed/quotes/nls/cco CCO +3.71%  is forecast to report a loss of a penny a share in the third quarter.

Coty Inc. /quotes/zigman/16536112/delayed/quotes/nls/coty COTY -0.79%  is projected to post first-quarter earnings of 29 cents a share.

Scripps Networks /quotes/zigman/511428/delayed/quotes/nls/sni SNI +0.46%  is expected to report third-quarter earnings of 84 cents a share.

Wendy’s Co. /quotes/zigman/7917415/delayed/quotes/nls/wen WEN +3.60%  is likely to report earnings of 6 cents a share in the third quarter.

Sotheby’s /quotes/zigman/241211/delayed/quotes/nls/bid BID +2.27%  is projected to report a loss of 47 cents a share in the third quarter.

Nvidia Corp. /quotes/zigman/80597/delayed/quotes/nls/nvda NVDA +6.98%  is expected to post third-quarter earnings of 19 cents a share.

Late Wednesday, Qualcomm Inc. /quotes/zigman/77257/delayed/quotes/nls/qcom QCOM +0.54%  said its fiscal fourth-quarter earnings rose to 86 cents a share from 73 cents a share a year earlier. On an adjusted basis, the chip maker earned $1.05 a share, below Wall Street’s forecast of $1.09 a share. Qualcomm shares skidded over 4% in after hours.

Activision Blizzard Inc. /quotes/zigman/110164/delayed/quotes/nls/atvi ATVI +1.39%  on Wednesday said its third-quarter profit fell to 5 cents a share from 20 cents a share a year ago while adjusted earnings slid to 8 cents a share versus 15 cents a share. Shares of Activision slid 1.9% in after hours.
