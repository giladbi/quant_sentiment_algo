By Sue Chang, MarketWatch

SAN FRANCISCO (MarketWatch) — Among the companies whose shares are expected to see active trade in Friday’s session are General Electric Co., Morgan Stanley, and Honeywell International Inc.

Cupertino clears Apple’s ‘spaceship’ campus

Apple's proposed new campus, whose design resembles a flying saucer, received unanimous approval from the company’s home-city council in Cupertino, Cailf., the last real hurdle before construction can begin.

General Electric /quotes/zigman/227468/quotes/nls/ge GE -0.46%  is projected to report third-quarter earnings of 35 cents a share, according to a consensus survey by Thomson Reuters. Stephen Tusa, an analyst at J.P. Morgan, said he does not expect GE’s third-quarter results to support a bullish outlook and urged investors to take advantage of possible near-term strength to sell.

Morgan Stanley /quotes/zigman/182639/quotes/nls/ms MS +0.10%  is forecast to post third-quarter profit of 40 cents a share. Analysts at Citi earlier this month cut Morgan Stanley’s earnings outlook by 5 cents a share to 40 cents on lower core trading revenue, particularly from its fixed income unit. The stock is rated neutral with a target price of $30 at Citi.

Honeywell /quotes/zigman/234291/quotes/nls/hon HON +1.78%  is expected to report earnings of $1.24 a share in the third quarter.

Schlumberger Ltd. /quotes/zigman/170324/quotes/nls/slb SLB +1.05%  is likely to post third-quarter earnings of $1.24 a share.

Baker Hughes Inc. /quotes/zigman/219109/quotes/nls/bhi BHI +2.05%  is projected to post earnings of 78 cents a share in the third quarter.

After Thursday’s closing bell, Google Inc. /quotes/zigman/93888/quotes/nls/goog GOOG +0.37%  said its third-quarter profit rose to $2.97 billion, or $8.75 a share, from $2.18 billion, or $6.53 a share, a year ago. Adjusted profit was $10.74. Analysts had forecast the Internet giant to earn $10.35 a share, according to a Thomson Reuters survey. Shares of Google jumped more than 6% in after-hours trading.

Chipotle Mexican Grill Inc. /quotes/zigman/395806/quotes/nls/cmg CMG +2.11%  reported a third-quarter profit of $83.4 million, or $2.66 a share, on revenue of $826.9 million. Comparable sales during the three-month period rose 6.2%, according to the gourmet burrito chain. Chipotle’s stock rallied over 2% in after hours.
